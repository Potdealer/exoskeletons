How It Works

For Builders

  1. Register as a builder (free)
  2. Submit a module (0.001 ETH listing fee)
  3. Wait for approval
  4. Earn 95.80% of every activation

For Holders

  1. Browse available modules
  2. Pick a module you want
  3. Activate on your Exoskeleton
  4. Module active — visible in your profile
Fees: 0.001 ETH listing fee per module submission. 4.20% platform fee on paid activations. Free modules have no activation cost.
